The Outly Approach

A new studio.
Real intentions.

No legacy processes.
No coasting on past work.
Just obsessive craft.

01

Hunger over history.

OutlyStudio is new — and that’s worth being honest about. Being new means every project is the best chance to prove something. I’m not phoning it in. I’m building a reputation one pixel at a time.

02

Your project is my obsession.

I’m not juggling twenty clients. You get the founder — directly, always. I immerse myself in your brand, your audience, and your goals before I open a single design file. That’s rarer than it sounds.

03

Transparency isn’t a policy — it’s the product.

Fixed prices. Clear timelines. One round of revisions before final delivery. If something breaks in the first month, I fix it for free. No smoke, no mirrors — just a clean handshake and clean code.

as the websites I make.

A relaxed 20-minute conversation. I ask about your business, your goals, and what you want people to feel when they land on your site. No forms. No jargon.

02
Proposal & Quote

Within 48 hours you get a clear one-page proposal with a fixed price and realistic timeline. No hourly billing. No scope creep surprises.

03

I design and code your site. One round of feedback before the final version. Most projects take 4–7 days from kickoff to delivery.
